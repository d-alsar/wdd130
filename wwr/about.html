Rafting Travel

Our purpose is to inspire a sense of adventure, foster camaraderie, and promote environmental stewardship through the power of outdoor exploration. Whether you're seeking an adrenaline-packed escapade or a tranquil float surrounded by breathtaking scenery, our rafting trips cater to all levels of adventurers. Join us on the water and let the currents carry you to unforgettable moments and lifelong memories.

History

In the heart of the Appalachian Mountains, a group of friends founded Cascade Expeditions in the late 1980s, igniting a passion for whitewater rafting in River's Edge. What began as a humble venture evolved into a renowned rafting company, offering thrilling adventures and unforgettable experiences on the region's wildest rivers, embodying the spirit of exploration and camaraderie. Today, Rafting Travel continues to inspire adventurers, keeping the legacy of its founders alive with every paddle stroke and exhilarating rapid.
