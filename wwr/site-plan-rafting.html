Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to inspire a sense of adventure, foster camaraderie, and promote environmental stewardship through the power of outdoor exploration. Whether you're seeking an adrenaline-packed escapade or a tranquil float surrounded by breathtaking scenery, our rafting trips cater to all levels of adventurers. Join us on the water and let the currents carry you to unforgettable moments and lifelong memories.

Audience

Our trips cater to all levels, from thrilling Class IV rapids for pros to scenic floats for beginners, with experienced guides ensuring your safety and enjoyment. You'll experience picturesque landscapes and diverse wildlife, including bald eagles, otters, and sometimes black bears, surrounded by lush forests, cliffs, and waterfalls. Rafting fosters teamwork as participants must communicate, trust, and work together to navigate the rapids. Our guides facilitate fun team-building activities on and off the water, creating lasting memories.
